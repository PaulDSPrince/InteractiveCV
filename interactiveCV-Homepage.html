Stratolaunch
The worlds largest plane, designed to carry rockets containing satellites or other payloads, into a low Earth orbit.
The rocket will be mounted on the central wing of the plane and be carried up to a high atmospheric altitude. This reduces the amount of fuel and parts needed to overcome gravity by the rocket as the lift that the planes wings generate create a lot of the upward forces.
The plane itself will release the rocket high in the atmosphere and then return to land as a more standard plane would. This also means that the plane is recoverable and reusable saving money over more traditional launch stages of rockets that can't be reused.
Once released from the plane, the rocket can adjust its orbit, using its own thrusters and then release its payload into its desired position before deorbiting itself if needed.
